// how it works

Our method in brief.

We design by subtraction, not addition.

  • Listen. To the site, the light and the way you actually live.
  • Reduce. Until only the load bearing ideas remain.
  • Detail. Every joint and edge resolved with care.
  • Build. With makers who share the same standard.

// what to remember

Three rules of restraint.

Simple, but easy to forget on a busy project.

  • One idea. Each space carries a single clear intention.
  • Honest material. Show what a thing is made of, never fake it.
  • Earn the edge. Every line on the drawing must justify itself.
