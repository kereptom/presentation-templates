// how it works

Digital onboarding in short.

The flow does not wait at a counter, it follows the customer.

  • Captures. Identity and documents straight from the phone camera.
  • Verifies. Against trusted sources in seconds, not days.
  • Scores. Risk and eligibility before the first reply.
  • Activates. The account is ready to use the same evening.

// two paths

Branch-first, or mobile-first.

Same customer, two approaches and two cost curves.

Branch-firstEverything runs through a teller. Personal but slow and costly.
ScheduledAppointments and callbacks. Better, yet still tied to hours.
Mobile-firstSelf-service in the app, help on demand. Lowest cost to serve.
HybridHigh-value advice in branch, routine tasks in the app.

// what to keep in mind

Three rules of rollout.

Simple, but easy to forget.

  • One owner. Every feature has the name of a person who is accountable.
  • A measurable goal. You know in advance how you will recognize success.
  • A human approves. For high-risk steps a person always makes the call.
