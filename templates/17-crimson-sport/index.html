// how it works

Smart load management in brief.

The plan does not chase fatigue, it predicts it.

  • Tracks. Heart rate, sleep and effort from key sessions.
  • Learns. What preceded past overuse and slumps.
  • Warns. Before form drops or injury shows up.
  • Plans. Recovery into the window when it costs the least.

// what to keep in mind

Three rules of rollout.

Simple, but easy to forget.

  • One owner. Every tool has the name of a person who answers for it.
  • A measurable goal. You know in advance how you will judge success.
  • A human decides. On critical steps a person always makes the call.
