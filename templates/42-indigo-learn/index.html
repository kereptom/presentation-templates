// how it works

Adaptive learning in short.

The path bends to the learner, not the other way around.

  • Senses. What each learner already knows and where they stall.
  • Adapts. Difficulty and pace to keep the challenge just right.
  • Nudges. With timely reminders before motivation fades.
  • Reports. Progress that mentors can actually act on.

// two paths

Self-paced, or cohort-based.

Same content, two formats and two very different finish rates.

Self-pacedLearn anytime. Flexible, but completion often quietly stalls.
ScheduledFixed weekly releases. More rhythm, less personal flexibility.
Cohort-basedLearn together on a shared timeline. Highest completion.
BlendedLive sessions plus self-paced work between them.

// things to keep in mind

Three rules of rollout.

Simple, but easy to forget under deadline pressure.

  • One owner. Every course has the name of a person accountable for it.
  • A measurable goal. You decide upfront how you will know it worked.
  • A human in the loop. For high-stakes feedback, a mentor always reviews.
