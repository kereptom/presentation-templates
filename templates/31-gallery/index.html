// how it works

A new acquisition in brief.

An object becomes part of the story step by step.

  • Receive. Condition checked and photographed on arrival.
  • Research. Provenance, history and meaning traced.
  • Conserve. Stabilised and prepared for display.
  • Display. Placed where its story lands best.

Chronological, or thematic.

The same works, two ways to hang them.

ChronologicalWalk through time. Clear, but can feel like a list.
ThematicGroup by idea. Surprising connections, harder to navigate.
MonographicOne artist in depth. Rich, but narrow in scope.
DialogueOld and new face to face. Fresh, needs strong framing.

// what to keep in mind

Three rules of display.

Simple, but easy to forget.

  • One story. Each room answers a single clear question.
  • Room to breathe. Empty wall is part of the design, not waste.
  • The visitor decides. Let people find their own path through the work.
