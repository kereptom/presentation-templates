// how it works

Good play, in short.

A toy earns its place through what it invites, not what it does.

  • Invites. Open-ended play with no single right answer.
  • Grows. New layers as the child gets older and bolder.
  • Connects. Moments shared with a sibling, friend or parent.
  • Lasts. Built to survive the floor, the bath and the years.

// keep in mind

Three rules of play.

Simple, but easy to forget on a deadline.

  • One clear joy. Each toy promises one thing and delivers it well.
  • Test with kids. Real children decide, not the boardroom.
  • Safety first. Nothing ships until it passes every check.
