// how it works

The growth loop in short.

Each active user creates the next one.

  • Acquire. New users land through a clear, low-friction entry.
  • Activate. They hit the first value moment fast.
  • Retain. A habit forms before the trial ends.
  • Refer. Happy users invite the next cohort.

// two motions

Self-serve, or sales-led.

Same product, two ways to reach the market.

Self-serveUsers sign up and pay alone. Cheap to scale, thin per deal.
Sales-ledReps close larger accounts. Higher cost, bigger contracts.
Product-led salesFree usage warms leads before a rep ever calls.
HybridSelf-serve for small teams, sales for enterprise.

// things to keep in mind

Three rules of fundraising.

Simple, but easy to forget under pressure.

  • Raise on traction. Numbers tell the story before the pitch does.
  • Know your runway. You always know how many months of cash remain.
  • Pick the right partner. The investor outlasts most of your features.
